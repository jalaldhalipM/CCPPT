The Core Challenge

Why Career Compass Is Needed

Students often study without passion or purpose. They follow trends without knowing whether that path suits them. Career Compass steps in to solve this problem by giving clarity before a student takes the next step.

01

Studying Without Passion

Many students find themselves stuck in courses that don't align with their natural interests, leading to a lack of motivation and purpose.

02

Following Trends Blindly

Students often chase "hot" careers based on market hype rather than personal fit, resulting in poor long-term performance and satisfaction.

03

Clarity Before Action

WeLe Career Compass provides the missing direction, ensuring students understand their path before committing time and resources.
